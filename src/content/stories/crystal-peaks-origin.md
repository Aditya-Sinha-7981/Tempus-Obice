Long ago, before the first age of men, a celestial star fell from the heavens and struck the mountain peaks. Where it landed, the stone shattered into a thousand luminous shards, each carrying the essence of the cosmos.

The mountain folk discovered that these crystals could hold memory—every emotion, every thought, every dream that touched them was preserved for eternity.

Today, scholars from distant lands travel to the Crystal Peaks seeking wisdom from the ancient stones. But the mountains keep their secrets close, revealing knowledge only to those deemed worthy.

The hum you hear when standing among the crystals is said to be the whispers of a thousand years of accumulated memory, waiting for someone who can truly listen.
